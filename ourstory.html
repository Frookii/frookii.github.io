Our Story


We at Frookii are a passionate software design and development team inspired by a culture of play and learning. Together our team aims to bring quality experiences to children growing up in a digital world. Through our experience and research into the development of software for children, Frookii aspires to be a leader in producing trustworthy games and experiences that inspire the confidence of both children and parents alike.
